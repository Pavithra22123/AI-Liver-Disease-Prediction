About Us

Pioneering AI-Powered Liver Disease Detection

Our Mission

At LiverAI, we are committed to revolutionizing liver disease diagnosis through cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology. Our mission is to provide accessible, accurate, and early detection tools that empower healthcare professionals and patients to make informed decisions about liver health.

We believe that early detection saves lives. By combining advanced machine learning algorithms with medical expertise, we aim to reduce the burden of liver disease worldwide and improve patient outcomes through timely intervention.

What We Do

Our AI-powered system offers two comprehensive prediction methods:

  • Blood Test Analysis: Advanced machine learning algorithms analyze clinical parameters including bilirubin levels, liver enzymes (SGPT/SGOT), protein levels, and more to predict liver disease risk with 95% accuracy.
  • Medical Image Analysis: Deep learning models powered by MobileNet architecture analyze liver scan images to detect fibrosis stages (F0-F4) with 92% accuracy.
  • Comprehensive Risk Assessment: Provides detailed health scores, risk classifications, and personalized medical recommendations.
  • Instant Results: Get predictions in seconds with detailed explanations and actionable insights.
