Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to LiverAI's Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and medical information. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our Liver Disease Prediction System.

Your Trust Matters: We understand that medical data is highly sensitive. We implement industry-standard security measures and comply with applicable data protection regulations to safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number
  • Emergency contact information (guardian name and phone)
  • Account credentials (encrypted passwords)

2. Medical Data

  • Blood test parameters (bilirubin, liver enzymes, proteins, etc.)
  • Medical scan images (liver images for fibrosis detection)
  • Prediction results and health scores
  • Test history and timestamps

3. Technical Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Session data and cookies
  • Usage patterns and interaction data

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Medical Analysis: To perform AI-powered liver disease predictions and provide health assessments
  • Account Management: To create and manage your user account, authentication, and preferences
  • Communication: To send you test results, updates, and important notifications
  • Service Improvement: To enhance our AI models and improve prediction accuracy (anonymized data only)
  • Research: To conduct medical research and statistical analysis (anonymized and aggregated data)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest
  • Password Protection: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit data access to authorized personnel only
  • Secure Storage: Medical data is stored on secure, HIPAA-compliant servers
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Data Backup: Regular backups ensure data availability and disaster recovery

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal or medical data. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Healthcare Providers: With your explicit consent to share results with your doctor
  • Emergency Contacts: Guardian information may be used in case of critical health alerts
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services (cloud hosting, analytics) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Research Partners: Only anonymized and aggregated data for medical research purposes

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal and medical data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Medical records are retained for a minimum period as required by law (typically 7 years). You may request account deletion at any time, after which your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, maintain sessions, and analyze usage patterns. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Essential cookies required for system functionality cannot be disabled.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately for deletion.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via email or prominent notice on our website. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
